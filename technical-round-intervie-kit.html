Calmness & Confidence Tips

Handle Unknown Questions

Frame it positively: "That's a great question. While I haven't used that specific tool, my experience with [related concept] suggests I would..."

Think Aloud

Articulate your thought process. It shows the interviewer how you approach problems, even if you don't know the final answer immediately.
